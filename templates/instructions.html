Instructions

Follow the on-screen instructions to perform the movement. Ensure your camera is properly positioned.

Step 1: Choose body part

- Depends on what body part you want to do exercise.

Step 2: Select types of body movements.

- Refer to nurse.

For elbow:

- Extension.

- Flexion.

For shoulder:

- Extension.

- Flexion.

- Adduction.

- Abduction.

Step 3: Enter target angle.

- Put the angle that refer to psychiatrist then submit.

Step 4: Move the body part.

1. Camera will open and there will appear 3 green dots with lines.

2. It will show the current angle.

3. Try to move the body part until it reaches the angle.

4. When you are done, whether you have reached your target or not, press the back button on the website.

Step 5: Stop camera

- Press stop camera button.

Step 6: View activities.

- You will see the activities you have done.

Step 7: Consult with doctor

- Get advice from a psychiatrist.
